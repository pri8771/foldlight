## Slide 2: The Problem

**Hook:** Mobile puzzle games are stagnant.

- The #1 mobile genre is puzzle games ($8.4B market in 2024)
- Top titles (Candy Crush, Wordle) rely on simple mechanics from 10+ years ago
- Players crave depth: 67% of mobile gamers say they want "more complex puzzles"
- Discovery problem: 95% of puzzle games look and feel identical
- Retention crisis: Average Day-30 retention for puzzle games is only 4–7%

**The Gap:** No premium-feeling, mechanically innovative puzzle game exists at scale on iOS.

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution

**Foldlight** is the first spatial folding puzzle game on iOS.

### Core Innovation
- **The Fold Mechanic:** Players fold sections of a magical glass-paper board so tiles overlap, combine, and interact
- **Emergent Gameplay:** 7 tile interaction types create thousands of unique puzzle configurations
- **World Narrative:** Each solved puzzle restores a broken world — emotional investment beyond just "next level"

### Why Now
- iOS 17 SpriteKit + SwiftUI maturity enables fluid, beautiful 2D animation
- ASMR/satisfying content drives organic discovery (TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
- Premium design quality differentiates in a sea of templated mobile games

## Slide 4: Product Demo

### Core Loop
1. Player sees broken biome (dark, fractured board)
2. Player strategically folds sections of the board
3. Tile combinations create solutions (light bends, paths connect, monsters captured)
4. Light reaches GoalCrystal → puzzle solved
5. Light Fragments collected → World restored (meta-progression)

### Key Screens
- **World Map:** 10 biomes, each with 50–500 levels, visible restoration progress
- **Puzzle Canvas:** Tactile folding with satisfying animations and sound design
- **Cosmetic Shop:** Themes, board skins, tile packs — all purely cosmetic
- **Achievement Center:** 200+ achievements, leaderboards, Daily Challenges

### Differentiators
- Procedurally infinite levels (algorithmic generation post-Story)
- ASMR-quality fold animations with Doppler sound effects
- Offline-first: zero network dependency during gameplay

## Appendix A: Technical Architecture Summary

- **Stack:** Swift 5.9 / SwiftUI / SpriteKit / SwiftData / StoreKit 2
- **Min Target:** iOS 17.0
- **Architecture:** Clean Architecture + MVVM
- **Offline-First:** Zero network calls during gameplay
- **Asset Pipeline:** Figma → SVG → SpriteKit Atlas
- **Procedural Engine:** Reverse-construction puzzle generator
- **Performance:** 60fps target on iPhone 12+, 120fps on ProMotion devices

## Appendix B: Monetization Philosophy

**We will never sell:**
- Level skips
- Extra moves
- Unfair difficulty curves to force purchases

**We will sell:**
- Cosmetics (board themes, tile skins)
- Convenience (extra hint packs)
- Content bundles (new biomes at fair price)

**Player trust is our #1 asset.**
